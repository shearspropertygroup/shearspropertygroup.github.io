Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our process, timelines, offers, and partnerships with homeowners and investors.

What types of properties do you buy?

We buy a wide range of properties across the United States, including single-family homes, condos, townhomes, and small multifamily properties, in any condition.

How fast can you close?

Closings can occur in as little as 7-14 days after acceptance, depending on title work and lender requirements.

Do I need to make repairs before selling?

No repairs are required. We buy properties as-is and handle most post-purchase renovations.

Are there fees or commissions?

There are no broker commissions. Offers are straightforward, with clear terms and disclosures up front.

What information do you need to start?

Property address, recent photos, and a brief description of condition and ownership timeline are typically needed.
